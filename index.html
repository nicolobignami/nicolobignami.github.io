I am a PhD student at SISSA, Trieste, in the group Geometry and Mathematical Physics. My advisors are Alina Marian (ICTP) and Andrea Ricolfi (SISSA). I am broadly interested in the geometry of moduli spaces of sheaves. My current research focuses on Quot schemes on surfaces and on moduli spaces of vector bundles on hyperkähler varieties.
